What we do

Help

If you are a Beginner, Advanced, Expert or in need of just asking a question, here at Social Gaming we are here to help. We help people find their next favourite game or if they have already found it, then help learn the basics. We help everyone by putting beginners with experts when having fun and when competing have the option for beginners to watch.

Teach

Teaching people is what we are their for. We will be at the events teaching people who are completely new to the game. Even our experts that go to the events are there to teach people the quicker way and right way of doing certain things. We hold sessions where if there is a particular game you want attention to, then we will help with that.

Build

We build a community. A community where people from all over the UK and the world, come and experience a game together. We help build relationships and even build people's confidence when experiencing an event for the first time. We even build... litterally build. We build nintendo switches, PS4, Xbox, PC anything we can get our hands on.
